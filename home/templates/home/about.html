About Us

Here at Chirpy Chooks we take pride in raising quality poultry while adhering to the highest standards of animal welfare. This not only makes for happy, healthy and productive birds, but also for satisfied customers and easy poultry management with great rewards.

Delivery and Pick-Up information

As our products are live animals, we are unable to offer shipment via postal service or courier.
We do however offer an arranged home delivery service for counties Limerick, Clare and Galway. Once your order is close to completion, you will be contacted to arrange a delivery date.

For customers located outside of the specified counties, pick-up is available during our opening hours at the Chirpy Chooks farm. You will be notified once your order is ready for collection.

Contact Us

Please get in touch with us for any queries regarding your order, delivery/pick-up or general product information.
We are also happy to offer advice before or after your purchase with us. If you can't find your questions answered in our Forum, please reach out and we'll be glad to help!
